## Minimal Version (Recommended)

**Privacy Policy**

We collect and store chat conversations to provide customer service. Chat logs are stored in Supabase. We may collect your name and email if you request follow-up. For questions or to delete your data, contact us.

---

## Slightly More Detailed (Still Simple)

**Privacy Policy**

**Data We Collect:**
- Chat messages and conversation history (stored in Supabase)
- Session IDs
- IP addresses (for security)
- Name and email (only if you request follow-up)

**How We Use It:**
- To provide customer service
- To improve our chatbot
- To follow up if you request it

**Data Storage:**
- Chat logs stored in Supabase database
- Data retained for customer service purposes

**Your Rights:**
- Request access to your data
- Request deletion of your data
- Contact us with privacy questions

**Contact:**
[Your email or contact form]

## Even Simpler (One Sentence)

**Privacy Policy:** Chat logs are stored in Supabase for customer service. Contact us to delete your data.

## Recommended Approach

Use the **Minimal Version** above. It's:
- ✅ Legally sufficient (discloses what you collect)
- ✅ Easy to understand
- ✅ Short (users will actually read it)
- ✅ Covers the basics

You can always expand it later if needed, but this covers the essentials.
